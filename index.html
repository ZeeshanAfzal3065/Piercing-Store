Piercing Aftercare Balm

Pierce Aura Balm is a gentle, skin-safe formula for all types of piercings, including sensitive areas like the tragus. It helps soothe irritation, reduce redness, and protect against infection, without harsh chemicals. The balm supports natural healing and promotes faster, safer recovery.

Directions for Use:

Apply a small amount of balm to the pierced area 3 to 4 times daily, depending on the severity and sensitivity of the skin. Regular use promotes clean, calm, and properly healed piercings

Important Aftercare Reminder:

Do not remove piercing studs during the healing period. Premature removal can cause irritation, infection, or closure of the piercing.

Recommended Healing Times:

– Earlobe: 8 weeks (2 months)

– Nose: 12 weeks (3 months)

– Cartilage: 16 weeks (4 months)

– Tragus: 16 weeks (4 months)

– Belly: 12 weeks (3 months)
